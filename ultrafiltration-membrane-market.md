<p><p>The ultrafiltration membrane market is segmented into two main types: inorganic membrane and organic membrane. Inorganic membranes are made of materials like ceramics or metals, offering high mechanical strength and chemical resistance. On the other hand, organic membranes are made of polymers such as polyethersulfone or polyacrylonitrile, providing flexibility and ease of fabrication. Both types of membranes are widely used in water treatment, food and beverage processing, pharmaceuticals, and other industries for separating particles and impurities from liquids through a filtration process.</p></p>
