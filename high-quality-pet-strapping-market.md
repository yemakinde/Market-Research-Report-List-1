<p><strong>The High Quality PET Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embossed PET Strapping</li><li>Smooth PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Quality PET Strapping has two main market types: embossed PET strapping and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping is characterized by its textured surface, which provides increased grip and friction to secure heavy loads during transportation. On the other hand, smooth PET strapping is known for its smooth surface finish, making it ideal for use with automatic strapping machines due to its consistent feeding and tensioning capabilities. Both types of PET strapping offer excellent tensile strength and durability for a wide range of packaging and bundling applications.</p></p>
