<p><p>The computer fan market consists of two main types: AC fans and DC fans. AC fans are powered by alternating current electricity and are commonly used in larger computer systems or industrial applications. On the other hand, DC fans are powered by direct current electricity, making them more energy efficient and popular in smaller devices like laptops or personal computers. Both types of fans play a critical role in cooling computer systems and preventing overheating.</p></p>
