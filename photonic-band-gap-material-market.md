<p><strong>The Photonic Band-gap Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1-D Photonic Crystals</li><li>2-D Photonic Crystals</li><li>3-D Photonic Crystals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photonic band-gap material market is segmented into three types based on the structure of the crystals - 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D photonic crystals. 1-D photonic crystals consist of periodic layers with variations in refractive index along one direction, while 2-D photonic crystals have variations in two directions creating a flat lattice. On the other hand, 3-D photonic crystals have variations in all three directions, leading to a complete photonic band gap. Each type offers unique properties and applications in the field of photonics.</p></p>
