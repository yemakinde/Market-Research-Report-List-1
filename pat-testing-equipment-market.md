<p><p>The PAT Testing Equipment market encompasses various types of testing equipment used to ensure the safety and functionality of electrical appliances. Fail/Pass PAT Testing determines if the appliances meet safety standards, while Earth bond Testing checks the integrity of the appliance's protective grounding. Insulation Testing evaluates the electrical insulation of the appliances. Battery Powered Testing examines battery-powered appliances. Leakage/load Testing detects any potential leaks or excessive energy consumption. These different types cater to specific testing requirements and contribute to ensuring the overall safety of electrical appliances.</p></p>
