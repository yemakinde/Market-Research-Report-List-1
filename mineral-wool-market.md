<p><p>Mineral wool, also known as rock wool or slag wool, is a versatile insulation material used in construction and industrial applications. There are two main types of mineral wool on the market: glass wool and stone wool. Glass wool is made from recycled glass and is known for its excellent thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Stone wool, on the other hand, is made from volcanic rock and is prized for its fire resistance and durability. Both types of mineral wool offer superior protection against heat, sound, and fire, making them popular choices in the construction industry.</p></p>
