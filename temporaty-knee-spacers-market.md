Cement spacers are made of antibiotic-impregnated cement, metal spacers are made of stainless steel or titanium, and other types of spacers can include antibiotic-loaded polymethylmethacrylate (PMMA) beads or prefabricated spacers. Each type of spacer has its own advantages and limitations in terms of infection control and mechanical properties.</p></p>
