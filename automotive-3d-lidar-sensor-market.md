<p><strong>The Automotive 3D LiDAR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical LiDAR uses moving parts to scan the environment by emitting laser pulses and measuring their reflection off objects. This type of LiDAR is known for its high resolution and long range capabilities, making it ideal for autonomous vehicles. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR does not use any moving parts and instead relies on micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) to steer laser beams. Solid State LiDAR is more compact, reliable, and cost-effective, making it suitable for mass production and integration into various automotive applications.</p></p>
