<p><strong>The Aluminum Cookware Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Aluminum Cookware</li><li>Anodized Aluminum Cookware</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast aluminum cookware is made by pouring molten aluminum into a mold, resulting in a lightweight and durable product with excellent heat distribution. On the other hand, anodized aluminum cookware undergoes a special electrochemical process that makes the surface harder and more scratch-resistant, as well as non-reactive with acidic foods. Both types of aluminum cookware are popular choices in the market due to their efficiency in cooking and longevity. Consumers can choose between cast aluminum cookware for its affordability and lightweight properties, or anodized aluminum cookware for its durability and non-stick surface.</p></p>
