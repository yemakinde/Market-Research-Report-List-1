<p><p>The automotive spring market can be categorized into several types including coil springs, leaf springs, lowered springs, and others. Coil springs are spiral-shaped springs that provide stability and support to a vehicle's suspension system. Leaf springs consist of several layers of metal strips to absorb shocks and distribute weight. Lowered springs are designed to lower a vehicle's ride height for improved handling and aesthetics. Other types of automotive springs include torsion bars, air springs, and stabilizer bars that contribute to the overall performance and comfort of a vehicle.</p></p>
