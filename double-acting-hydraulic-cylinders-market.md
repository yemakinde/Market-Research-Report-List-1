<p><strong>The Double-Acting Hydraulic Cylinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Double-acting hydraulic cylinders are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their construction and functionality. Type I cylinders have a single rod attached to a piston that moves back and forth within the cylinder, providing force in both directions. In contrast, Type II cylinders have two rods attached to both ends of the piston, allowing for independent movement in both directions. This distinction affects the applications and efficiency of the cylinders in various industries.</p></p>
