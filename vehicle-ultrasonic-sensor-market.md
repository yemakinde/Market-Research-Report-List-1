<p><p>The Vehicle Ultrasonic Sensor Market includes two main types: Forward Sensors and Reversing Sensors. Forward Sensors are designed to help drivers navigate and park their vehicles by detecting obstacles in front of them. Reversing Sensors, on the other hand, aid drivers in backing up safely by alerting them to any obstacles behind their vehicle. Both types of sensors utilize ultrasonic technology to provide accurate distance measurements and help prevent accidents while driving or parking.</p></p>
