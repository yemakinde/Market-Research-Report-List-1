<p><p>Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) are electrical devices used to protect sensitive electronic equipment from voltage surges or spikes. There are three main types of SPDs in the market: voltage switch type, pressure limiting type, and combination type. Voltage switch type SPDs detect excessive voltage and divert it away from the equipment. Pressure limiting type SPDs absorb the surge energy, reducing the voltage to a safe level. Combination type SPDs combine both features, providing a comprehensive protection solution. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in safeguarding electronic equipment.</p></p>
