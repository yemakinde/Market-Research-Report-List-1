<p><p>Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) devices consist of various types. The first is the test strips, which are small disposable sticks that are used to collect a blood sample for glucose measurement. The second is the glucose meter, a handheld device that analyzes the blood sample and displays the glucose levels. Lastly, lancets are small, sharp needles used to prick the skin and obtain the blood sample. These three components work together to enable individuals with diabetes to monitor their blood glucose levels at home, providing them with valuable information for managing their condition.</p></p>
