<p><p>In-Line Torque Transducers are sensors used to measure torque in rotating systems. There are two main types in the market - Static Torque Sensors and Dynamic Torque Sensors. Static Torque Sensors are designed for stationary or slow-moving systems, providing accurate measurements at a fixed point. Dynamic Torque Sensors are suitable for high-speed rotating systems, capturing real-time data during operation. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring efficient and safe operation of machinery.</p></p>
