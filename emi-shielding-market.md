<p><p>EMI shielding refers to the practice of protecting electronic devices from electromagnetic interference. The market for EMI shielding encompasses various types of products. EMI shielding tapes and laminates are adhesive materials that provide a barrier against electromagnetic waves. Conductive coatings and paints are applied to surfaces to enable efficient grounding and prevent interference. Metal shielding involves enclosing devices with metallic materials to block electromagnetic signals. Conductive polymers offer flexible and lightweight shielding solutions. EMI/EMC filters are electronic components designed to suppress or filter out electromagnetic interference in electrical systems.</p></p>
