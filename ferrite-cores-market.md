<p><p>Ferrite cores are used in various applications such as transformers and inductors. The market for ferrite cores can be categorized into three main types: Nickel-Zinc Ferrite Core, Mn-Zn Ferrite Core, and Others. Nickel-Zinc Ferrite Cores are known for their high frequency characteristics and low losses, making them suitable for applications in telecommunications. Mn-Zn Ferrite Cores are preferred for low to medium frequency applications due to their high permeability and saturation levels. The Others category includes specialty ferrite cores with unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
