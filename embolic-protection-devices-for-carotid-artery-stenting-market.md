<p><p>Embolic Protection Devices for Carotid Artery Stenting market includes two main types - Distal Occlusion Devices and Proximal Occlusion Devices. Distal Occlusion Devices are placed beyond the stenotic lesion to capture any embolic debris during the stenting procedure. Proximal Occlusion Devices are placed at the ostium of the common carotid artery to prevent emboli from traveling to the brain during the stenting procedure. These devices play a crucial role in reducing the risk of stroke and improving patient outcomes in carotid artery stenting procedures.</p></p>
