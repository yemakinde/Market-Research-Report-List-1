<p><p>Gel-filled fiber cables are used in both single-mode and multi-mode markets. Single-mode cables are designed for long-distance, high-speed data transmission over single strands of glass fiber, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. Multi-mode cables, on the other hand, are better suited for shorter distances and lower bandwidth applications. They use multiple strands of glass fiber to allow for easier alignment of light signals. Both types of cables are essential for efficient and reliable communication networks.</p></p>
