<p><p>Hemodialysis disposables and accessories market consists of various types of products essential for the hemodialysis procedure. Dialyzer plays a crucial role in filtering waste and excess fluid from the blood. AV Fistula and AV Graft are used as access points for connecting patients to the dialysis machine. Bloodlines are tubes that carry blood to and from the dialyzer. Catheters are used for temporary vascular access. Transfer sets are used to connect different components of the dialysis machine. Other accessories include needles, syringes, and tubing needed for the hemodialysis process.</p></p>
