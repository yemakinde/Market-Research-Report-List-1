<p><p>Manual seeders are agricultural tools used for sowing seeds manually. Mechanical manual seeders operate by turning a crank, which then rotates the seed drum and dispenses seeds. Pneumatic manual seeders use compressed air to release seeds as the operator applies pressure to the handle. Other types of manual seeders may include handheld seeders, push seeders, or dial seeders, which offer different methods of seed distribution. These various market types cater to different farmer preferences and specific agricultural requirements.</p></p>
