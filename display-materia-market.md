It is commonly used in TVs, monitors, and smartphones. On the other hand, OLED (organic light-emitting diode) technology uses organic compounds to produce light and does not require a backlight, resulting in thinner and more energy-efficient displays. OLED is widely used in high-end smartphones and TVs.</p></p>
