<p><p>Suction-side pool cleaners come in two main types: wheeled and without wheel. Wheeled suction-side cleaners utilize wheels to navigate around the pool, providing better stability and maneuverability. On the other hand, suction-side cleaners without wheels rely on their suction power to move around the pool, offering a more lightweight and simple design. Both types of cleaners effectively remove dirt and debris from the pool, but the choice between wheeled and wheel-less options will depend on factors such as pool size, shape, and personal preference.</p></p>
