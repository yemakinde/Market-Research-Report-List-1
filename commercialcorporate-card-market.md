<p><p>Open-loop cards are typically branded with major card networks such as Visa or Mastercard and are accepted at a wide range of merchants. They offer greater flexibility and are widely used for general spending purposes. Closed-loop cards, on the other hand, are restricted to a specific merchant or group of merchants and are typically used for loyalty programs, gift cards, or employee incentives. While closed-loop cards offer limited usability, they often come with special promotions or rewards specific to the issuing merchant.</p></p>
