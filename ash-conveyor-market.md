<p><ul><li>Drag Chain Conveyor</li><li>Screw Conveyor</li><li>Submerged Drag Conveyors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ash conveyors are commonly used in power plants and industrial settings to transport ash produced from combustion processes. Different types of ash conveyors include drag chain conveyors, screw conveyors, submerged drag conveyors, and others. Drag chain conveyors are ideal for handling heavy and abrasive materials, while screw conveyors are better suited for lighter materials. Submerged drag conveyors are used for transporting ash in water-filled troughs. Each type of conveyor has its own unique advantages and applications in the ash handling industry.</p></p>
