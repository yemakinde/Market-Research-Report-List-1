<p><p>Non-alcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH) biomarkers market includes various types such as serum biomarkers, cytokeratin-18 (CK 18) marker, inflammatory markers, and others. Serum biomarkers are substances in the blood that indicate the presence of NASH. CK 18 marker specifically measures liver cell death, which is common in NASH patients. Inflammatory markers assess inflammation levels in the liver, which is a key indicator of NASH progression. Other biomarkers may include genetic markers or imaging techniques for diagnosing and monitoring NASH.</p></p>
