<p><p>Wind turbine composite materials market can be categorized into two major types based on resin used - Thermosetting Resin and Thermoplastic Resin. Thermosetting resin materials are widely used in wind turbine blades due to their excellent mechanical properties and resistance to heat. Thermoplastic resin materials, on the other hand, offer advantages such as recyclability and ease of processing. Both types play a crucial role in the manufacturing of wind turbine components, contributing to the growth of the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
