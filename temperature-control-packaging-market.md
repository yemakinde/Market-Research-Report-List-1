<p><p>Temperature control packaging market offers a variety of types, including bubble and composite materials. On the other hand, composite materials combine different layers of materials such as foils, films, and insulating materials to create a barrier against temperature fluctuations. Both types are widely used in industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and biotechnology to ensure the safe transportation of temperature-sensitive goods.</p></p>
