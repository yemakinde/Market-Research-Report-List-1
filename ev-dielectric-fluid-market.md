<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EV Dielectric Fluid market is divided into two main types based on viscosity - Low Viscosity and High Viscosity. Low Viscosity dielectric fluids are thinner and flow easily, making them suitable for applications where rapid heat dissipation is essential. On the other hand, High Viscosity dielectric fluids are thicker and provide better insulation properties, making them ideal for applications where enhanced dielectric strength and stability are required. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of electric vehicle components.</p></p>
