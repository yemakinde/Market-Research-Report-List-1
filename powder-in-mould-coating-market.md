<p><p>Powder in-mould coating is commonly used in the manufacturing of Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Molding Compound (BMC) products. SMC consists of thermosetting resins, glass fibers, and fillers, while BMC is made up of short fibers, resin, fillers, and additives. Both types of technologies offer enhanced surface finish, durability, and resistance to corrosion. SMC is typically used in automotive and construction industries, while BMC is commonly applied in electrical and appliance applications. Both SMC and BMC markets are growing due to their superior properties and versatility.</p></p>
