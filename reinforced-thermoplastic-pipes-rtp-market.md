<p><p>Reinforced Thermoplastic Pipes (RTP) are used in various industries for transferring fluids under high pressure. The market includes two types: Non-metallic RTP and Metallic RTP. Non-metallic RTP pipes are made of composite materials such as aramid fibers or glass fibers, offering high corrosion resistance and flexibility. On the other hand, Metallic RTP pipes have steel reinforcements, providing higher strength and durability. Both types of RTP pipes are preferred for their lightweight nature, ease of installation, and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.</p></p>
