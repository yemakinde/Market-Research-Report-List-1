<p><p>Thermal fillers are a type of material used in various industries for insulation purposes. The market for thermal fillers can be categorized into different types based on the composition of the fillers. Silicone-based fillers are made from silicone polymers and offer excellent thermal stability and low toxicity. Alumina-based fillers, on the other hand, are composed of alumina oxide particles and provide high thermal conductivity and resistance to harsh conditions.
