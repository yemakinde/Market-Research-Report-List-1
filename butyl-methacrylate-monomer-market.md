<p><p>The Butyl Methacrylate Monomer market can be divided into three types based on the structure of the butyl group attached to the methacrylate molecule. These types are n-Butyl Methacrylate, i-Butyl Methacrylate, and Others. n-Butyl Methacrylate contains a straight chain butyl group, i-Butyl Methacrylate contains an isobutyl group, and Others include variations such as sec-butyl and tert-butyl groups. Each type of butyl group imparts different properties to the monomer, leading to a variety of applications in the market.</p></p>
