<p><p>Stainless Insulated Bottles come in two main types - Non-Vacuum and Vacuum. Non-Vacuum Stainless Insulated Bottles have a single stainless steel wall that helps to insulate the liquid inside to keep it at a desired temperature. On the other hand, Vacuum Stainless Insulated Bottles are double-walled with a vacuum-sealed space in between, which provides better insulation and temperature retention. Vacuum bottles are generally considered to be more effective at maintaining the temperature of hot or cold beverages for longer periods of time.</p></p>
