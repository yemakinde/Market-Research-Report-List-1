<p><p>Moisture curing adhesives are classified into four main types: polyurethane, silicone, cyanoacrylate, and polyolefin. </p><p>Polyurethane adhesives provide strong bonding for a wide range of materials, including metals, plastics, and wood. Silicone adhesives are known for their flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. Cyanoacrylate adhesives, commonly known as super glue, offer fast bonding for quick applications. Polyolefin adhesives are ideal for bonding low-energy surfaces like polyethylene and polypropylene.</p><p>Each type of adhesive has unique properties that cater to specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
