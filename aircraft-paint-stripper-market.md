<p><p>Aircraft paint strippers are classified into three main types: the caustic type, the acidic type, and the solvent type. The caustic type uses strong alkaline chemicals to break down paint, while the acidic type utilizes acids to remove paint. The solvent type, on the other hand, relies on organic solvents to dissolve paint. Each type has its own set of advantages and disadvantages in terms of effectiveness, safety, and environmental impact, catering to different preferences and requirements in the aircraft industry.</p></p>
