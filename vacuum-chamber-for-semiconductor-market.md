<p><p>A vacuum chamber is essential in the semiconductor industry, particularly in the processes of Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Etching. Both processes require a vacuum environment to ensure purity and precision in the deposition and etching processes. Vacuum chambers help maintain the necessary low-pressure environment for these applications, enabling the production of high-quality semiconductor devices.</p></p>
