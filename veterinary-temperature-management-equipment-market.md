<p><strong>The Veterinary Temperature Management Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Warmers</li><li>Convection Warming Systems</li><li>Conduction Warming Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary temperature management equipment market includes various types of equipment used to maintain and regulate body temperature in animals. Fluid warmers are devices that warm intravenous fluids before administration to prevent hypothermia. Convection warming systems use forced air to warm animals during surgery or post-operative care. Conduction warming systems involve the use of warming pads or blankets to transfer heat directly to the animal's body. These equipment types are crucial in veterinary medicine to ensure proper temperature management and promote the well-being of animals during medical procedures.</p></p>
