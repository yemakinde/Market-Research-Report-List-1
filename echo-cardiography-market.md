<p><p>Echocardiography is a non-invasive test that uses sound waves to produce images of the heart. There are different types of echocardiography markets, including M-mode, which provides a single-dimensional view of the heart's movement; two-dimensional (2-D or B-mode), which offers a more detailed image of the heart in real time; and Doppler, which shows blood flow through the heart and blood vessels. Each type of echocardiography market has its own unique benefits and uses in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions.</p></p>
