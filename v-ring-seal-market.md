<p><p>A V-ring seal is a type of axial lip seal that is commonly used to provide effective sealing in rotary applications. <p><p>The V-ring seal market is classified into four main types: VA, VS, VL, and VE. VA type seals are commonly used for rotating shafts, providing axial sealing. VS type seals are used for protecting bearings from contaminants and retaining lubricants. VL type seals are designed for static applications requiring efficient sealing. VE type seals are used in combination with other seals for enhanced protection against various contaminants. Each type caters to specific sealing requirements in different industrial applications.</p></p>
