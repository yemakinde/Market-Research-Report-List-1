<p><strong>The Hydraulic Components for Construction Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Element</li><li>Actuator</li><li>Control Element</li><li>Auxiliary Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic components for construction machinery market can be divided into four main types: power element, actuator, control element, and auxiliary components. The power element includes hydraulic pumps and motors that generate the fluid power needed to operate the machinery. Actuators, such as cylinders and valves, use this fluid power to move and control different parts of the machine. Control elements regulate the flow and pressure of the hydraulic fluid, ensuring smooth operation. Auxiliary components, like filters and reservoirs, support the overall functionality of the hydraulic system.</p></p>
