<p><strong>The Metal & Glass Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal & Glass Coatings market is segmented into three types: Solventborne, Waterborne, and UV coatings. Solventborne coatings contain volatile organic compounds and are known for their fast drying time. Waterborne coatings are environmentally friendly with low VOC content and easy clean-up. UV coatings are cured instantly using ultraviolet light, providing high resistance to scratches and chemicals. Each type offers unique advantages catering to different applications and preferences in the metal and glass industry.</p></p>
