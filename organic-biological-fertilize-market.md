<p><p>Organic biological fertilizers are categorized into different types based on their key functions. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers help plants absorb nitrogen from the atmosphere. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers aid in releasing phosphorus from insoluble compounds in the soil. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers enhance the availability of potassium for plant uptake. Other types of organic biological fertilizers may include micronutrient-rich formulations or those that promote beneficial microbial activity in the soil. Each type of fertilizer plays a specific role in supporting plant growth and improving soil health.</p></p>
