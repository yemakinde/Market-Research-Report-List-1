<p><p>Pleated membrane filters are available in various types such as PP (Polypropylene) filters, PES (Polyethersulfone) filters, PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) filters, Nylon filters, and others. PP filters are cost-effective and ideal for general filtration applications. PES filters offer high flow rates and low protein binding for critical applications. PTFE filters are chemically resistant and suitable for aggressive chemicals. Nylon filters are durable and compatible with a wide range of solvents. Other types of pleated membrane filters cater to specific filtration needs in various industries.</p></p>
