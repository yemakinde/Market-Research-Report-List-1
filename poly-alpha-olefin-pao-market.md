<p><p>Poly Alpha Olefin (PAO) is a type of synthetic oil that is commonly used in automotive and industrial lubricants due to its excellent thermal and oxidative stability. There are three main types of PAO markets based on viscosity: low viscosity PAO, medium viscosity PAO, and high viscosity PAO. Low viscosity PAO is preferred for applications requiring high fluidity, while medium viscosity PAO offers a balance between flow and lubrication properties. High viscosity PAO is used in heavy-duty applications where high load bearing capacity is required.</p></p>
