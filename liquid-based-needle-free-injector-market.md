<p><p>Liquid-Based Needle Free Injector market consists of four main types: Jet, Spring, Laser, and Vibration injectors. Jet injectors use high-pressure air to deliver liquid medication through the skin. Spring injectors use a spring mechanism to create pressure for injection. Laser injectors utilize laser technology to create microscopic pores in the skin for medication delivery. Vibration injectors use vibration to deliver medication through the skin. Each type of injector offers different benefits in terms of injection speed, pain, and accuracy in medication delivery.</p></p>
