<p><p>The Wound Care Biologics Market consists of three major types: Biologic Skin Substitutes, Enzyme Based Formulations, and Growth Factors. Biologic Skin Substitutes are synthetic or naturally derived materials that can be used as alternatives to grafting for wound healing. Enzyme Based Formulations consist of enzymes that aid in the debridement and cleansing of wounds. Growth Factors are naturally occurring proteins that promote cell growth and tissue repair. These different types of wound care biologics offer diverse approaches to advancing wound healing and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
