<p><p>The Manufacturing Execution System (MES) software market includes two main types: Point MES and Integrated MES. Point MES refers to software solutions that focus on specific functionalities within the manufacturing process, such as quality control or inventory management. Integrated MES, on the other hand, provides a comprehensive end-to-end solution, integrating various functionalities like production planning, scheduling, material tracking, and maintenance. Point MES is more suited for companies with specific needs, while Integrated MES caters to organizations looking for a holistic approach to optimize their entire manufacturing process.</p></p>
