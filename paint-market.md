<p><p>Paint market can be categorized into four main types: water-based paint, solvent-based paint, powder paint, and others. Water-based paint is environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based paint is durable and dries quickly, but can release harmful chemicals into the air. Powder paint is popular for industrial applications due to its durability and lack of solvent emissions.
