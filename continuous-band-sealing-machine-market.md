<p><p>Continuous band sealing machines are available in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical band sealers are designed for sealing bags standing upright, allowing for easy and efficient packaging of liquids or powders. Horizontal band sealers, on the other hand, are ideal for sealing bags lying flat on a conveyor belt, making them suitable for packaging solid items like snacks or hardware. Both types offer reliable and continuous sealing for various industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
