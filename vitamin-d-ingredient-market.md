<p><p>Vitamin D ingredient market consists of two main types: Vitamin D3 and Vitamin D2. Vitamin D3, also known as cholecalciferol, is derived from animal sources like fish oil and lanolin, and is the most bioavailable form for humans. On the other hand, Vitamin D2, also called ergocalciferol, is derived from plant sources such as mushrooms and yeast. While both types can be used to supplement Vitamin D levels, Vitamin D3 is considered more effective in raising blood levels of Vitamin D.</p></p>
