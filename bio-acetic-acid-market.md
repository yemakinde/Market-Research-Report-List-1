<p><ul><li>Grain and Sugar Fermentation</li><li>Wood Cellulose Fermentation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-acetic acid is produced through two main types of fermentation processes: grain and sugar fermentation, and wood cellulose fermentation. Grain and sugar fermentation involves using grains like corn or sugar sources like sugarcane to produce acetic acid. On the other hand, wood cellulose fermentation utilizes wood-based sources such as paper pulp or sawdust to generate acetic acid. Both methods offer sustainable alternatives to traditional petroleum-based acetic acid production, contributing to the growing bio-based market trend.</p></p>
