<p><ul><li>UV Offline Systems</li><li>VIS Offline Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolution Offline Systems market comprises UV Offline Systems and VIS Offline Systems. UV Offline Systems are designed to measure the dissolved substances using ultraviolet spectroscopy. It operates by passing UV light through the sample and measuring the absorbance of the UV light. On the other hand, VIS Offline Systems use visible spectroscopy to measure the dissolved substances. It involves passing visible light through the sample and measuring the absorbance of the visible light. Both UV Offline Systems and VIS Offline Systems are utilized in the dissolution testing process to determine the dissolution rate of solid dosage forms.</p></p>
