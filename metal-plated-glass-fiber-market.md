<p><p>Metal-plated glass fiber market types include galvanized fiberglass, aluminized fiberglass, silver plated fiberglass, nickel-plated fiberglass, and copper plated fiberglass. Galvanized fiberglass is coated with a layer of zinc for increased corrosion resistance. Aluminized fiberglass is coated with aluminum for thermal insulation. Silver plated fiberglass provides excellent conductivity. Nickel-plated fiberglass offers high strength and durability. Copper plated fiberglass is known for its electrical conductivity and corrosion resistance. Each type caters to different applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>
