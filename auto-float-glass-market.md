<p><p>Auto Float Glass Market includes two main types: Clear Float Glass and Green Float Glass. Clear Float Glass is a transparent glass that allows maximum light transmission and provides optical clarity. It is commonly used in automotive windshields and windows due to its high quality and durability. On the other hand, Green Float Glass has a light green tint due to the presence of iron oxide, providing some heat insulation properties. It is frequently used in buildings and vehicles to reduce glare and improve energy efficiency.</p></p>
