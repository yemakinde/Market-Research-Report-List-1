<p><strong>The Reflective Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Glass Bead Type Reflective Materials</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type Reflective Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective materials are categorized into two main types: Micro Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Micro Glass Bead Type materials consist of tiny glass beads that reflect light back to its source, providing visibility in low light conditions. On the other hand, Micro Prismatic Type materials utilize small prisms to reflect and refract light, enhancing visibility from various angles. Both types are commonly used in road signs, safety vests, and other applications where visibility is essential for safety and visibility.</p></p>
