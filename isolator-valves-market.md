<p><p>The isolator valves market comprises various types of valves, including ball valves, butterfly valves, gate valves, globe valves, and other similar products. Ball valves consist of a spherical disc that controls the flow of fluids or gases, while butterfly valves use a disc to regulate the flow. Gate valves use a gate or wedge-shaped disc to control the fluid flow, and globe valves utilize a plug or disc to manage the flow. Apart from these popular types, there are other valves available in the market that serve the same purpose of isolating or controlling fluid or gas flow.</p></p>
