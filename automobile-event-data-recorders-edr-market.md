<p><p>Automobile Event Data Recorders (EDRs) are devices installed in vehicles to record data related to vehicle operation and performance. There are two main types of EDR markets: Discrete EDR and Integrated EDR. Discrete EDRs are separate devices that can be added to a vehicle, while Integrated EDRs are built-in systems that are already installed in the vehicle by the manufacturer. Both types of EDRs serve the same purpose of capturing and storing crucial data in the event of an accident or other incident.</p></p>
