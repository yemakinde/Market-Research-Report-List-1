<p><p>The vehicle downpipe market can be classified into two main types: catted and catless downpipes. Catted downpipes are equipped with catalytic converters, which help reduce harmful emissions. They are typically preferred by environmentally conscious consumers and those looking to comply with emissions regulations. On the other hand, catless downpipes do not have catalytic converters, which can result in better performance but may not meet emissions standards. This type is often favored by enthusiasts looking to maximize power and upgrade their exhaust systems.</p></p>
