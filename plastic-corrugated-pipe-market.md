<p><strong>The Plastic Corrugated Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wall Corrugated</li><li>Double Wall Corrugated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic corrugated pipe market is divided into two main types: Single Wall Corrugated and Double Wall Corrugated. Single Wall Corrugated pipes are lightweight and flexible, making them suitable for non-pressurized applications like drainage, agricultural, and septic systems. On the other hand, Double Wall Corrugated pipes have an outer wall for strength and an inner wall for smoothness, making them ideal for pressurized applications such as storm sewers, culverts, and sanitary sewers. Both types offer durability, corrosion resistance, and easy installation.</p></p>
