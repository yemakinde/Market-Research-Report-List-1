<p><p>Asphalt modifiers can be categorized into three main types: physical modifiers, chemical modifiers, and other modifiers. Physical modifiers, such as polymers and elastomers, are added to improve the physical properties of asphalt, such as flexibility and durability. Chemical modifiers, like additives and rejuvenators, enhance the chemical properties of asphalt, such as aging resistance and adhesion. Other modifiers include warm mix additives and anti-stripping agents, which provide additional functionalities to the asphalt. Each type of modifier plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance of asphalt pavement.</p></p>
