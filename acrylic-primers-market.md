<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Non-Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic primers are available in two main types: water-based and non-water-based. Water-based acrylic primers are easier to clean up and have lower levels of VOCs, making them more environmentally friendly. They are suitable for interior applications and dry quickly. Non-water-based acrylic primers are more durable and resistant to moisture, making them ideal for exterior use and in high-humidity environments. They provide a strong bond and better adhesion to surfaces, but may have higher levels of VOCs and require proper ventilation during application.</p></p>
