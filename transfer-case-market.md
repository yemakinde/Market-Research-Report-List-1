<p><p>Transfer case market can be classified into two types based on the driving mechanism: Gear driven and Chain driven. Gear driven transfer cases use a set of gears to transfer power from the transmission to the front and rear axles, providing a more durable and reliable operation. On the other hand, chain driven transfer cases utilize a chain to transfer power, offering smoother operation and quieter performance. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and preferences of the users.</p></p>
