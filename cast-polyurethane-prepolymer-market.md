<p><p>Cast Polyurethane Prepolymer market is divided into two main types - Polyester Based and Polyether Based. Polyester based prepolymers offer high resistance to chemicals and hydrolysis, making them ideal for applications in industries such as oil and gas, automotive, and aerospace. On the other hand, Polyether based prepolymers provide excellent resistance to abrasion and tear, making them suitable for applications in industries such as mining, construction, and marine. Both types offer unique properties and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
