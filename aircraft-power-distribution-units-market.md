<p><p>Primary Power Distribution Unit (PDU) in the aircraft power distribution units market refers to the main unit responsible for distributing power from the aircraft's power source to various systems and components. It ensures the efficient and reliable distribution of power throughout the aircraft. On the other hand, Secondary Power Distribution Unit (SDU) serves as a backup to the primary unit, providing additional support in case of system failures or emergencies. Both units play a crucial role in maintaining the overall functionality and safety of the aircraft's power systems.</p></p>
