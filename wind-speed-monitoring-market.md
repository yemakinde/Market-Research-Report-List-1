<p><p>Wind speed monitoring market is divided into two types: short-term monitoring and long-term monitoring. This type of monitoring is often used for activities such as weather forecasting, outdoor events planning, and construction operations. On the other hand, long-term monitoring involves continuously measuring wind speed over an extended period, which could range from several weeks to years. Long-term monitoring is essential for analyzing wind patterns, optimizing renewable energy systems, and conducting environmental studies.</p></p>
