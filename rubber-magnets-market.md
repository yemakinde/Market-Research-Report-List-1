<p><p>Rubber Magnets are classified into two main types based on their magnetic properties: Isotropic and Anisotropic. Isotropic Rubber Magnets have a uniform magnetic field in all directions, making them suitable for applications where the magnet can be oriented in any way. On the other hand, Anisotropic Rubber Magnets have a specific orientation of magnetic particles, providing a stronger magnetic force in a particular direction. Both types have their own unique strengths and applications in industries such as automotive, advertising, and signage.</p></p>
