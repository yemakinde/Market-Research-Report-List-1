<p><p>Automotive aluminum casting market includes several types such as die casting, permanent mold casting, sand casting, and others. Die casting involves injecting molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure. Permanent mold casting uses reusable molds made from steel or iron. Sand casting involves creating a mold by compacting sand around a pattern. Other methods may include investment casting or squeeze casting. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of cost, production speed, and design flexibility for automotive manufacturers.</p></p>
