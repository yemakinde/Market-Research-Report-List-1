<p><strong>The Laminated Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fusion Method Laminated Steel</li><li>Bonding Agent Laminated Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminated steel is available in two main types: fusion method laminated steel and bonding agent laminated steel. Fusion method laminated steel is created by using heat and pressure to fuse multiple layers of steel together, resulting in a strong and durable material. Bonding agent laminated steel, on the other hand, involves using adhesives or bonding agents to combine the steel layers. Both types of laminated steel offer unique properties and are used in various industries for their strength, flexibility, and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
