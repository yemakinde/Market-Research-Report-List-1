<p><p>Automotive Plain Carbon ERW Tubes include two main types in the market: Straight Seam Welded Pipe and Spiral Welded Pipe. Straight Seam Welded Pipe features a longitudinally welded seam, offering high strength and uniform thickness. On the other hand, Spiral Welded Pipe has a helical seam, providing increased flexibility and the ability to withstand high pressure. Both types are commonly used in automotive applications due to their durability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of manufacturing.</p></p>
