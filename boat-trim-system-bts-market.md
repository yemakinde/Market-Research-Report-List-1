<p><strong>The Boat Trim System (BTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat Trim System (BTS) comprises trim tabs and interceptors, which are both types of systems used to adjust the trim of a boat in order to optimize its performance and stability. Trim tabs are attached to the transom of the boat and can be adjusted manually or electronically to control the boat's pitch and roll. Interceptors are mounted on the hull and provide a similar function by adjusting the lift of the boat in different sea conditions. Both types of systems are essential for maintaining control and efficiency while boating.</p></p>
