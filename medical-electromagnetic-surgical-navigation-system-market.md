<p><ul><li>Hybrid Navigation</li><li>Electromagnetic Navigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market consists of two types: Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic Navigation. </p><p>Hybrid Navigation involves the integration of different techniques like electromagnetic tracking, optical tracking, and inertial sensing to provide accurate guidance and visualization during surgical procedures. This type of system is suitable for complex surgeries where real-time information and precise navigation are required.</p><p>On the other hand, Electromagnetic Navigation solely relies on electromagnetic fields to track the position and movement of medical instruments and devices within the patient's body. This navigation system is used in minimally invasive procedures to enhance accuracy and reduce the risk of complications.</p></p>
