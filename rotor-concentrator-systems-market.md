<p><p>Rotor concentrator systems are air pollution control devices used to capture and concentrate volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from industrial emissions before they are released into the atmosphere. These systems use rotating discs or cylinders coated in a specialized adsorbent material to capture VOCs, which are then desorbed and treated in a thermal oxidizer.</p><p>The Rotor Concentrator Systems Market is witnessing steady growth due to increasing regulations on air quality and emissions control in various industries.
