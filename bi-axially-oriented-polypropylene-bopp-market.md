<p><strong>The Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat film strength method</li><li>Tubular quench method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is a type of plastic film that is produced through two different methods - flat film strength method and tubular quench method. The flat film strength method involves stretching the film in both machine and transverse directions, resulting in improved strength and clarity. On the other hand, the tubular quench method involves blowing a tube of molten polypropylene which is then stretched in both directions. Both methods are commonly used in the packaging industry for applications such as food packaging and labeling.</p></p>
