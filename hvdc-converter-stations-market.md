<p><strong>The HVDC Converter Stations Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Bi-Polar</li><li>Back to Back</li><li>Multi-Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVDC Converter Stations Market Types include Monopolar, Bi-Polar, Back to Back, and Multi-Terminal systems. Monopolar systems have only one conductor for both positive and negative poles. Bi-Polar systems have two conductors for positive and negative poles. Back to Back systems are used to connect two asynchronous AC systems. Multi-Terminal systems have more than two terminals for connecting multiple AC systems. These different types cater to varying needs and requirements in the transmission of high voltage direct current.</p></p>
