<p><strong>The Inhalation Therapy Nebulizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebulizers</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebulizers</li><li>Mesh Nebulizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inhalation therapy nebulizer market consists of three main types: pneumatic nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to create aerosol particles. Mesh nebulizers have a vibrating mesh membrane that produces fine droplets of medication. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of size, portability, efficiency, and breath-actuation, catering to different preferences and needs of patients undergoing inhalation therapy.</p></p>
