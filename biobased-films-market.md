<p><p>Biobased films are categorized into different market types, such as PBAT films, PLA films, PHB films, and others. PBAT films are bio-based and compostable, ideal for packaging applications. PLA films are derived from renewable resources like corn starch, offering biodegradable and recyclable qualities. PHB films are fully biodegradable and produced from microorganisms. Other biobased films utilize various biopolymers and natural materials to offer sustainable packaging solutions. Each type of biobased film serves a unique purpose in reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainability.</p></p>
