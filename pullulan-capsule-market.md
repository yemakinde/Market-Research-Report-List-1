<p><p>Pullulan capsules are a type of vegetarian capsules made from a natural, water-soluble polysaccharide called pullulan. These capsules are widely used in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries as an alternative to gelatin capsules. Pullulan capsules have excellent oxygen barrier properties, low moisture content, and can effectively mask the taste and odor of active ingredients.</p><p>The pullulan capsule market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. Increasing consumer preference for vegetarian and clean-label products is driving the demand for pullulan capsules. Moreover, the growing awareness regarding the harmful effects of animal-based capsules and the increasing adoption of vegan lifestyles are further fueling market growth.</p><p>In addition, the pharmaceutical industry's shift toward plant-based solutions is contributing to the market's expansion. Pullulan capsules offer an attractive packaging option for various oral drugs and dietary supplements, enhancing product stability and shelf life.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements in capsule manufacturing techniques and increasing research and development activities to explore new applications of pullulan capsules are expected to drive market growth.
