<p><p>Waterborne polyurethane adhesives market can be broadly categorized into three types based on their chemical composition: anionic, cationic, and nonionic. Anionic type adhesives are charged negatively, cationic type adhesives are charged positively, while nonionic type adhesives have no charge. Each type has its own set of properties and applications, making them suitable for various industries and end-uses. The choice of adhesive type depends on factors like substrates, curing conditions, and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
