<p><p>Inhalation sedatives are a type of medical gas used to induce sedation during surgeries or medical procedures. There are different types available in the market. Sevoflurane, Desflurane, Isoflurane, and Nitrous Oxide are four commonly used inhalation sedatives. Sevoflurane is known for its rapid onset and minimal irritation to airways. Desflurane has a fast recovery time and low metabolism in the body. Isoflurane is known for its stability and good muscle relaxation properties. Nitrous Oxide, also known as laughing gas, provides analgesic and sedative effects.</p></p>
