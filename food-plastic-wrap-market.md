<p><p>Food plastic wrap market includes different types such as PE, PVC, PVDC, and others. PE (Polyethylene) is the most commonly used plastic wrap due to its flexibility and durability. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers superior cling but has environmental concerns. PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) provides excellent barrier properties but is less commonly used. Other types include PP (Polypropylene) and PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) which offer varying degrees of flexibility and barrier properties. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits in the food packaging industry.</p></p>
