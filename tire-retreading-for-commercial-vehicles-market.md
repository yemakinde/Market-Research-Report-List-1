<p><strong>The Tire Retreading for Commercial Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tire retreading for commercial vehicles involves two main market types: Pre Cure and Mold Cure. Pre Cure retreading involves applying a new tread rubber to the tire casing before curing it in a chamber, while Mold Cure retreading uses a pre-molded tread rubber strip that is applied to the casing and then cured in a mold. Both methods provide cost-effective solutions for extending the lifespan of commercial vehicle tires by replacing worn treads, making them a popular choice for fleet managers looking to save money on tire replacement costs.</p></p>
