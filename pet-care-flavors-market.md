<p><ul><li>Water Soluble Flavors</li><li>Oil Soluble Flavors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water soluble flavors are those that can dissolve in water-based products such as pet food and treats. These flavors are easily absorbed by the pet's taste buds, providing a delicious and appetizing experience. On the other hand, oil soluble flavors are designed to be used in oil-based products like pet supplements and grooming products. These flavors are more concentrated and can provide a richer taste profile. Both types of flavors cater to the diverse needs of pet owners looking to enhance their pets' enjoyment of various products.</p></p>
