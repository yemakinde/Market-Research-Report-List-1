<p><p>Concrete surface treatment chemicals market includes curing compounds, mold release agents, and sealants. Curing compounds are used to retain moisture in the concrete during the early stages of curing to ensure strength and durability. Mold release agents are applied to molds to prevent concrete from sticking during the curing process. Sealants are used to protect the concrete surface from water, chemicals, and abrasion. Each type of chemical plays a crucial role in ensuring high-quality and long-lasting concrete surfaces.</p></p>
