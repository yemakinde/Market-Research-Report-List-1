<p><p>The seismic isolation floor systems market includes various types of isolation technologies such as Lead Rubber Isolator, Cross Linear Bearing, Damping Cylinder Device, and others. Lead Rubber Isolator systems use lead cores surrounded by rubber material to dampen seismic energy. Cross Linear Bearing systems involve sliding mechanisms to absorb and dissipate earthquake forces. Damping Cylinder Device systems utilize hydraulic cylinders to absorb vibrations and prevent structural damage. Other isolation technologies may include a combination of different materials and methods to enhance seismic resistance in buildings and structures.</p></p>
