<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Ultrasonic Microscope</li><li>Scanning Ultrasonic Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic microscopes are of two main types: digital ultrasonic microscope and scanning ultrasonic microscope. Digital ultrasonic microscopes use digital technology to capture and analyze images of samples at a microscopic level, providing high-resolution images for analysis and research purposes. On the other hand, scanning ultrasonic microscopes use a scanning probe to create images of samples by scanning across the surface, allowing for detailed imaging of samples at a nanoscale level. Both types offer advanced imaging capabilities for various scientific and research applications.</p></p>
