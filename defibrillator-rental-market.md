<p><p>The defibrillator rental market consists of three types: Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs), Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), and Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs). AEDs are portable devices used to treat sudden cardiac arrest by delivering an electric shock to the heart. ICDs are implanted under the skin and continuously monitor the heart's rhythm, delivering an electric shock if an abnormal rhythm is detected. WCDs are wearable devices used by individuals at risk of sudden cardiac arrest, providing continuous monitoring and delivering a shock if necessary.</p></p>
