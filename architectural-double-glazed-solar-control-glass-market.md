<p><p>Architectural Double Glazed Solar Control Glass includes two main types - Absorbing Solar Control Glass and Reflective Solar Control Glass. Absorbing Solar Control Glass absorbs a portion of the solar energy and reduces heat transmission. Reflective Solar Control Glass, on the other hand, reflects a significant amount of solar radiation. Both types help in controlling solar heat gain and reducing the need for artificial cooling, making buildings more energy-efficient and comfortable. These products are popular in commercial and residential buildings for their sustainability and cost-saving benefits.</p></p>
