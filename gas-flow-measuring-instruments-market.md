<p><p>Gas flow measuring instruments play a crucial role in accurately measuring the flow rate of gas within various industries. The market for these instruments can be broadly categorized into three types - Orifice Measuring Instruments, Vortex Measuring Instruments, and Turbine Measuring Instruments. Orifice measuring instruments use a small hole to measure gas flow, Vortex measuring instruments detect vortices shed by a bluff body in the gas flow, and Turbine measuring instruments use a rotating turbine to measure gas flow. Each type offers its own advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
