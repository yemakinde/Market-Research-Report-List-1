<p><p>The vehicle safety seat market consists of four types of seats. The infant seat is designed for newborns and provides rear-facing protection. Lastly, the convertible seat can be adjusted to cater to different age groups and can be rear-facing or forward-facing, providing long-term usability. Overall, the market offers a variety of options to ensure the safety of children in vehicles.</p></p>
