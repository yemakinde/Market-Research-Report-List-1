This growth can be attributed to several factors. <p><strong>The Borehole Gravity Survey Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Gravity Gradiometer</li><li>The Relative Gravity Gradiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The borehole gravity survey services market comprises two main types: Absolute Gravity Gradiometer and Relative Gravity Gradiometer. The Absolute Gravity Gradiometer measures the Earth's gravitational force directly, providing precise gravity measurements. On the other hand, the Relative Gravity Gradiometer measures differences in gravity readings at different locations, allowing for comparative analysis and identifying anomalies in the subsurface. Both types offer valuable data for various industries such as oil and gas exploration, geothermal energy, and mineral exploration.</p></p>
