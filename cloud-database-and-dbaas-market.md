<p><p>Cloud database refers to a database that is hosted on a cloud computing platform and can be accessed and managed remotely through the internet. It allows users to focus on utilizing the database for their applications, while the service provider takes care of managing the underlying infrastructure, security, and backups.</p><p>The cloud database and DBaaS market have been experiencing significant growth in recent years. <p><p>Cloud database and DBaaS market types can be categorized into three main segments: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud. Public cloud databases are hosted and maintained by third-party providers, allowing organizations to access and utilize database services over the internet. Private cloud databases, on the other hand, are owned and managed by an organization, ensuring greater control and security. Hybrid cloud databases combine elements of both public and private clouds, offering flexibility and scalability by utilizing a combination of on-premises and cloud-based resources.</p></p>
