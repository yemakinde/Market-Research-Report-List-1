<p><strong>The Fan-Out Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Fan-Out Packaging</li><li>High-Density Fan-Out Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fan-Out packaging is a type of semiconductor packaging that offers better performance and efficiency compared to traditional packaging methods. Core Fan-Out Packaging involves placing the chips directly on the substrate without the need for a package. High-Density Fan-Out Packaging, on the other hand, involves stacking multiple chip layers in a compact manner to increase the overall density. Both types offer advantages such as improved thermal performance, shorter interconnect lengths, and higher signal speeds, making them popular choices in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
