<p><p>Veterinary auscultators come in two main types: single head and double head. Single head auscultators have one chest piece for listening to sounds such as heartbeats and lung sounds, making them simpler and more straightforward to use. On the other hand, double head auscultators have two chest pieces, allowing for more precise and detailed auscultation of different areas of the animal's body. Both types are important tools for veterinarians in diagnosing and monitoring the health of animals.</p></p>
