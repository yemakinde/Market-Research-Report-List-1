<p><p>Dipped polyester cords are categorized into five market types based on their flexibility and stiffness levels. Ultra soft cords are extremely flexible and pliable, catering to projects that require a high level of drape and movement. Soft cords offer a balance between flexibility and structure, suitable for a wide range of applications. Semi stiff cords provide more rigidity and shape retention, ideal for structured designs. Stiff cords are highly rigid and maintain their shape well, perfect for projects requiring strong support. Super stiff cords offer the highest level of stiffness and support, best suited for projects needing substantial structure and form.</p></p>
