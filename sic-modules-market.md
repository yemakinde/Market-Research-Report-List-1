<p><p>SiC Modules market can be categorized into three main types: SiC MOSFET Type, SiC SBDs + SiC MOSFETs Type, and Other. SiC MOSFET Type refers to modules using Silicon Carbide Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors for high power applications. SiC SBDs + SiC MOSFETs Type combines Silicon Carbide Schottky Barrier Diodes with MOSFETs for improved efficiency and performance. The Other category includes modules using different SiC components such as JFETs, BJT, etc. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of power handling, efficiency, and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
