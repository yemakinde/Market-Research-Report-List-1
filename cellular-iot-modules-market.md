<p><ul><li>Cat M1</li><li>Cat NB1</li><li>GPRS</li><li>Cat NB2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellular IoT modules are communication devices used for connecting various IoT devices to cellular networks. There are different types of modules available in the market. Cat M1 modules provide faster data transmission, making them suitable for applications that require real-time response. Cat NB1 modules are designed for low-power applications with small data transmission needs. GPRS (General Packet Radio Service) modules support 2G networks and are typically used in areas with limited cellular infrastructure. Cat NB2 modules are an advanced version of Cat NB1, offering improved power efficiency and features for IoT applications.</p></p>
