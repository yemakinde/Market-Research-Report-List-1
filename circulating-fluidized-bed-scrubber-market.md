<p><p>The Circulating Fluidized Bed Scrubber market consists of two types: dry and semi-dry. In the dry market, the scrubbing process does not involve any liquid, and instead, dry sorbents are used to neutralize harmful gases. It is considered more cost-effective and easier to operate. On the other hand, the semi-dry market utilizes a combination of water and sorbents to remove pollutants. This type offers higher efficiency in capturing pollutants but requires more maintenance and operational costs compared to the dry market.</p></p>
