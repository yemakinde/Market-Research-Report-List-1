<p><p>Propeller de-icing systems are designed to prevent ice build-up on aircraft propellers, ensuring safe and efficient operation in icy conditions. There are three main types of propeller de-icing systems: mechanical de-icing systems, which use physical devices to remove ice; electric pulse anti-icing systems, which use electrical pulses to prevent ice formation; and electric heating anti-icing systems, which use heating elements to melt ice. Each type of system has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on aircraft requirements.</p></p>
