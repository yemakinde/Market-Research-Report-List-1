<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Magnetic</li><li>Infrared (IR)</li><li>Force Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive position sensors use changes in capacitance to determine the position of an object. Magnetic sensors utilize magnetic fields to track the movement. Infrared (IR) sensors detect the position using infrared light. Force sensors measure the force applied to determine the position. Other types include optical sensors, linear variable differential transformers (LVDT), and potentiometric sensors. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and environmental resistance for diverse applications in the two-wheeler market.</p></p>
