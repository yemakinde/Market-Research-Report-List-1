<p><p>In the invasive mechanical ventilators market, there are two types: stationary and portable. On the other hand, portable ventilators are more compact and lightweight, allowing mobility for patients. They can be easily transported and used outside of hospital settings, enabling patients to receive ventilatory support while on the move or at home.
