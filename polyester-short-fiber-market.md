<p><p>Polyester short fibers can be classified into two main types based on their structure- solid and hollow. Solid fibers are composed of a continuous solid strand of polyester material, which provides strength and durability. On the other hand, hollow fibers have a hollow core, which can provide insulation and moisture-wicking properties. Both solid and hollow fibers are widely used in the textile industry for manufacturing a variety of products such as clothing, home textiles, and industrial materials.</p></p>
