<p><p>MES (Manufacturing Execution System) software market can be categorized into two types: On-Premise and On-Cloud. On-Premise refers to software installations hosted locally on a company's servers and infrastructure, offering complete control and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, On-Cloud involves the software being hosted and accessed remotely via the internet, eliminating the need for local infrastructure and decreasing initial costs. It provides scalability, flexibility, and easier software updates, but may face security concerns and depend on internet connectivity.</p></p>
