<p><p>PVC waterstops are used in construction to prevent water leakage in concrete structures. The centerbulb type waterstop has a larger center bulb that provides a stronger barrier against water pressure. The dumbbell type waterstop has a rounded shape with two bulbs on either side, offering flexibility and better performance in joint movement. Both types are commonly used in different construction projects to ensure a watertight seal and protect structures from water damage.</p></p>
