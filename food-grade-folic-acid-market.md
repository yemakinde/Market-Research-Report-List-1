<p><p>Food Grade Folic Acid Market consists of three main types, including Dihydrofolate (DHF), Tetrahydrofolate (THF), and 5,10-methylenetetrahydrofolate (5, 10-MTHF). DHF is a precursor to THF, which is vital for the synthesis of DNA and RNA. THF is essential for cellular division and growth. 5, 10-MTHF plays a crucial role in the methylation cycle, converting homocysteine to methionine. These different types of folic acid serve distinct functions in the body and are important for overall health and wellness.</p></p>
