<p><p>Stainless steel powders for additive manufacturing are categorized into three main types based on their microstructure and composition: Austenitic Grade, Martensitic Grade, and Ferritic Grade. Austenitic Grade powders offer high corrosion resistance and are commonly used in industries such as aerospace and medical. Martensitic Grade powders provide superior hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for tooling and automotive applications. Ferritic Grade powders are known for their magnetic properties and good high-temperature performance, making them ideal for industrial applications.</p></p>
