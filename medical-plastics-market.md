<p><p>Medical plastics market is segmented into types such as Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), Polypropylene, Polyethylene, Plastic, and Silicone. PVC is widely used for IV bags and tubing due to its flexibility and chemical resistance. Polypropylene is commonly used for syringes and vials due to its transparency and high heat resistance. Polyethylene is used for medical packaging and containers due to its strong barrier properties. Silicone is used for medical implants and tubing due to its biocompatibility and durability in harsh conditions.</p></p>
