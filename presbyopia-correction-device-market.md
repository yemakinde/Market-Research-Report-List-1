<p><p>Presbyopia correction devices can be categorized into three main types: corneal inlays, scleral implants, and others. Corneal inlays are small lenses that are inserted into the cornea to improve near vision. Scleral implants involve the insertion of a small device into the sclera of the eye to help with focusing. Other types of presbyopia correction devices may include contact lenses, intraocular lenses, and surgical procedures such as LASIK. Each type of device offers a different approach to correcting presbyopia and improving near vision.</p></p>
