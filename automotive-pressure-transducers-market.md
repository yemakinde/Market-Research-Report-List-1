<p><strong>The Automotive Pressure Transducers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gauge Pressure</li><li>Absolute Pressure</li><li>Sealed Pressure</li><li>Vacuum Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Pressure Transducers are used to measure different types of pressure in vehicles. Gauge Pressure transducers measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, while Absolute Pressure transducers measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum. Sealed Pressure transducers measure pressure within a sealed system, and Vacuum Pressure transducers measure pressure below atmospheric pressure. Each type of pressure transducer is designed for specific applications in the automotive industry, providing accurate readings for various systems and components within vehicles.</p></p>
