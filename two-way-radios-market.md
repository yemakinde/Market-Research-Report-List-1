<p><p>Two-way radios come in different market types: analog and digital. Analog two-way radios are the traditional type that use frequency modulation to transmit and receive voice communications. They are cheaper and have a longer range compared to digital radios. On the other hand, digital two-way radios use digital modulation to encode and decode transmissions, offering clearer audio quality and enhanced privacy features. They are more expensive but provide better signal clarity and security, making them popular among businesses and organizations that require reliable communication tools.</p></p>
