<p><ul><li>Activator Adjuvants</li><li>Utility Adjuvants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Activator adjuvants are designed to enhance the effectiveness of pesticides by promoting better absorption, translocation, and overall performance of the active ingredient. Utility adjuvants, on the other hand, are mainly used to improve the physical properties of pesticide sprays, such as reducing drift, improving coverage, and increasing adhesion to plant surfaces. Both types of adjuvants play crucial roles in maximizing the efficacy and efficiency of agricultural spray applications, ultimately leading to improved crop protection and yield outcomes.</p></p>
