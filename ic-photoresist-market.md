<p><p>IC Photoresist, or integrated circuit photoresist, is a key component in the semiconductor manufacturing process. <p><p>IC photoresist market types include ArF Immersion, ArF Dry Film, KrF, G-LINE/I-LINE, and others. ArF Immersion photoresists are used in advanced lithography processes for sub-90nm circuit patterning. ArF Dry Film photoresists offer a cost-effective solution for mid-range resolutions. KrF photoresists are used for older technology nodes. G-LINE/I-LINE photoresists are suitable for larger feature sizes or more cost-sensitive applications. Other types of photoresists cater to niche markets or specific requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
