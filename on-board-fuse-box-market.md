<p><p>On-board fuse boxes are common in vehicles and are categorized into two main types: interior and under hood. Interior fuse boxes are typically located inside the vehicle, providing easy access for drivers and passengers. These boxes contain fuses that protect electrical components within the cabin, such as lights and air conditioning. On the other hand, under hood fuse boxes are usually found in the engine compartment and safeguard critical systems like the engine and fuel pump. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and functionality of a vehicle's electrical systems.</p></p>
