<p><p>Railway Vehicle Coupler Market Types include Automatic Couplers, Semi-Automatic Couplers, and Semi-Permanent Couplers. Automatic Couplers are fully automated and require no manual intervention for coupling and uncoupling. Semi-Automatic Couplers involve some manual effort but also include some automated features for easier operation. Semi-Permanent Couplers are typically used for longer-term connections and require manual effort for both coupling and uncoupling. Each type offers different levels of automation and ease of use depending on the specific needs of the railway system.</p></p>
