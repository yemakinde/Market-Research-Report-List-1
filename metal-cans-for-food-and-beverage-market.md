<p><p>Metal cans for food and beverage come in various market types such as three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by welding together three parts: the body, the bottom, and the top lid. They are commonly used for packaging food products. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are manufactured from a single piece of metal for both the body and the lid. They are often used for packaging beverages due to their seamless construction and ability to maintain product freshness.</p></p>
