<p><p>RTP Pipes, also known as Reinforced Thermoplastic Pipes, come in two main market types: non-metallic RTP and metallic RTP. Non-metallic RTP pipes are made from materials like high-density polyethylene, polyvinyl chloride, or polypropylene, providing corrosion resistance and flexibility. Metallic RTP pipes, on the other hand, are made with a steel or aluminum core for additional strength and pressure capabilities. Both types of RTP pipes are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, water distribution, and sewage systems for their durability and reliability.</p></p>
