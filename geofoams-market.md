<p><p>Geofoams are lightweight, cost-effective, and durable materials used in various construction applications. EPS Geofoams, made of expanded polystyrene, offer excellent thermal insulation and moisture resistance. XPS Geofoams, composed of extruded polystyrene, provide high compressive strength and resistance to moisture absorption. Both types of geofoams are widely used in road construction, embankments, landscaping, and slope stabilization projects. They are preferred for their ease of installation, ability to reduce settlement, and environmental sustainability. Geofoams help in reducing construction costs and overall project timelines.</p></p>
