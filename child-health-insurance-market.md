<p><p>Child health insurance can be categorized into two main market types: reimbursement type of medical expenses and medical subsidy type. In the reimbursement type, parents pay for their child's medical expenses and then seek reimbursement from the insurance company. Alternatively, in the medical subsidy type, the insurance company directly covers a portion of the child's medical expenses, reducing the financial burden on parents. Both market types aim to ensure children have access to necessary healthcare, but they differ in the way expenses are managed and reimbursed.</p></p>
