<p><p>Fiber optic cable assembly market consists of two main types: Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cable and Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cable. Single-mode fiber optic cable is designed for long-distance, high-speed data transmission with a small core size that allows light to travel straight down the fiber. Multi-mode fiber optic cable is better suited for shorter distances and can carry multiple light rays simultaneously due to its larger core size. Both types play a crucial role in providing high-speed and reliable communication networks for various industries.</p></p>
