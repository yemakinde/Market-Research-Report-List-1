<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Single Crystal Silicon Wafers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CZ Methods (Czochralski Method)</li><li>FZ Method (Floating Zone Method)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single crystal silicon wafers are produced using two main methods: CZ (Czochralski) and FZ (Floating Zone). CZ method involves melting silicon in a crucible and slowly pulling a single crystal ingot out, resulting in high-quality wafers. FZ method involves floating a zone of molten silicon through a rod to create a single crystal. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the semiconductor industry, with CZ method being more common for mass production and FZ method being preferred for specialized applications requiring ultra-pure silicon.</p></p>
