<p><p>Light curable adhesives market includes various types such as Acrylic Series, Silicon-gel Series, Anaerobic Series, and Others. Acrylic Series adhesives offer fast curing and high adhesion to a variety of substrates. Silicon-gel Series adhesives provide flexibility and thermal stability. Anaerobic Series adhesives are ideal for metal bonding as they cure in the absence of air. Other types of light curable adhesives include epoxy-based adhesives, polyurethane-based adhesives, and cyanoacrylate adhesives, each offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
