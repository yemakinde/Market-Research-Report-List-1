<p><p>Injection grouting materials are used for sealing, strengthening, and stabilizing structures. The four main types of injection grouting materials are polyurethane grouts, epoxy resin grouts, acrylate grouts, and other specialty grouting materials. Polyurethane grouts are flexible and used for sealing joints or cracks. Epoxy resin grouts are strong and used for structural bonding. Acrylate grouts are water resistant and used for soil stabilization. Other specialty grouting materials include sodium silicate grouts and microfine cement grouts for specific applications. Each type offers unique properties for different grouting needs.</p></p>
