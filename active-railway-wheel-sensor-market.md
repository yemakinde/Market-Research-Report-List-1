<p><p>The Active Railway Wheel Sensor Market includes two main types: Single Wheel Sensor and Double Wheel Sensor. Single Wheel Sensor systems are designed to monitor the speed and movement of individual wheels and can provide more precise data for maintenance and safety purposes. Double Wheel Sensor systems, on the other hand, are capable of simultaneously monitoring adjacent pairs of wheels, offering a more comprehensive view of train operation and allowing for more efficient fault detection and preventative maintenance strategies.</p></p>
