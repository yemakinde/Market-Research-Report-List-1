<p><p>Fire resistant glass is available in several types, including laminated, ceramic, tempered, and wired glass. Laminated glass consists of a layer of PVB interlayer between two or more glass sheets, providing fire protection by holding the glass together even when broken. Ceramic glass is made by incorporating ceramic materials into the glass mixture, increasing its resistance to fire. Tempered glass is heat-treated to improve its strength and thermal resistance. Wired glass has a wire mesh embedded within it, providing added reinforcement against fire.</p></p>
