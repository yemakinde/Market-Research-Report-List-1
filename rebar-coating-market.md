<p><ul><li>Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coated</li><li>Cement-polymer Composite Coated (CPCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rebar coating market types include Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coated and Cement-polymer Composite Coated (CPCC). Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coating is a thermosetting powder coating that provides corrosion protection to rebar. CPCC is a coating made of cement, polymer, and other additives that offer enhanced protection against corrosion and improve the bond between rebar and concrete. Both types of coatings are designed to extend the service life of reinforced concrete structures and ensure their durability in harsh environments.</p></p>
