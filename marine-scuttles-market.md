<p><p>Marine scuttles are divided into two main types: fixed marine scuttles and unfixed marine scuttles. Fixed marine scuttles are permanently installed in a specific location on a ship or vessel, providing a secure and reliable access point. On the other hand, unfixed marine scuttles are portable and can be moved to different locations as needed. Both types of marine scuttles serve the purpose of allowing access to different areas of a ship while maintaining safety and security standards.</p></p>
