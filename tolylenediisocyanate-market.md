<p><p>Tolylenediisocyanate (TDI) is commonly available in three market types: TDI 80/20, TDI 65/35, and TDI 100. TDI 80/20 contains 80% 2,4-tolylene disocyanate and 20% 2,6-tolylene disocyanate. TDI 65/35 contains 65% 2,4-tolylene disocyanate and 35% 2,6-tolylene disocyanate. TDI 100 is a single isomer product containing 100% 2,4-tolylene disocyanate. These market types offer varying compositions to suit different applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
