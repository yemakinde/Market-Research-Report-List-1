<p><p>Portable abrasive blasting equipment is widely used in various industries for cleaning, surface preparation, and finishing applications. There are two main types of portable blasting equipment: pressure and siphon. In the pressure market, compressed air forces abrasive material through a nozzle at high velocity, resulting in efficient and powerful cleaning. On the other hand, the siphon market uses a venturi system to create suction, pulling the abrasive material through the nozzle and propelling it onto the surface. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements and user preferences.</p></p>
