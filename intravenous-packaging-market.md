<p><p>Intravenous packaging refers to the packaging used for IV fluids and medications. There are two main types of intravenous packaging: medium barrier packaging and high barrier packaging. Medium barrier packaging offers a basic level of protection against external elements, while high barrier packaging provides superior protection against oxygen, moisture, and light. High barrier packaging is ideal for sensitive IV medications that are prone to degradation when exposed to air or light. Both types of packaging play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and effectiveness of intravenous products.</p></p>
