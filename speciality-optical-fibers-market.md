<p><p>Speciality optical fibers are used in various industries and applications such as telecommunications, medical, sensing, and defense. Multimode fibers have a larger core diameter compared to single-mode fibers, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through the fiber. They are commonly used for shorter distances and high-speed data transmission. Single-mode fibers have a smaller core diameter and allow only one mode of light to travel through the fiber, making them suitable for long-distance transmission and high-bandwidth applications.</p></p>
