<p><p>Flip-chip COB (Chip-On-Board) technology is a packaging technique that directly connects the bare integrated circuit chip to the substrate or circuit board without the use of wire bonding. Firstly, the increasing demand for smaller and faster electronic devices is driving the adoption of this technology. Flip-chip COB offers a higher density of interconnects and shorter signal paths, which result in improved performance and faster speeds.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for miniaturized electronic devices is propelling the market growth. Flip-chip COB technology allows for smaller form factors and enables higher component integration, which is essential for applications like smartphones, wearables, and IoT devices.</p><p>Moreover, the advantages offered by Flip-chip COB technology, such as improved thermal management, reduced power consumption, and improved reliability, are further contributing to its market growth.</p><p>In terms of market trends, the rising adoption of advanced packaging technologies, such as 3D IC integration and system-in-package (SiP), is creating new opportunities for the Flip-chip COB technology market.
