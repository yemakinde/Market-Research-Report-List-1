<p><strong>The Semi-Automatic Blast Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Blast Cabinet</li><li>Suction Blast Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-Automatic Blast Cabinets are available in two types: Pressure Blast Cabinets and Suction Blast Cabinets. Pressure Blast Cabinets utilize high-pressure systems to propel abrasive materials onto the surface being cleaned, providing efficient and precise cleaning for tough materials. Suction Blast Cabinets, on the other hand, use a suction system to pull abrasive materials through a gun and onto the surface, making them suitable for delicate and intricate cleaning tasks. Both types offer semi-automatic operation, enhancing productivity and accuracy in industrial cleaning applications.</p></p>
