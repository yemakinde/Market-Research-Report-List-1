<p><p>Curtain Wall Adhesives come in two main types: Acidic Adhesive and Neutral Adhesive. Acidic Adhesives are commonly used for bonding glass to metal or glass to glass in curtain wall systems. They have a higher level of adhesion and are more resistant to weathering. On the other hand, Neutral Adhesives are often preferred for bonding curtain wall components such as aluminum frames. They offer a more neutral pH balance, making them suitable for a wider range of materials and applications.</p></p>
