<p><p>CPVC pipe market types can be divided into Schedule 40 CPVC pipe and Schedule 80 CPVC pipe. Schedule 40 CPVC pipe is thinner and lighter, making it suitable for residential and light commercial applications. On the other hand, Schedule 80 CPVC pipe is thicker and more durable, ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications where higher pressure ratings are required. Both types of CPVC pipes are known for their corrosion resistance, ease of installation, and long service life, making them popular choices in various plumbing and industrial systems.</p></p>
